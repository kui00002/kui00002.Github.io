HTML Tags

<title></title>
-- the title of the webpage which will be displayed on top of the tab in a browser. Not on the page.

<main></main>
-- primary content of the page.

<nav></nav>
-- the navigation of the page, this will contain the links to other pages of the website.

<section></section>
-- group of related content on the page. A section must have its own heading.

<div></div>
-- used for single elements or short piece of content. Span shows the content inline with the rest of the content.
