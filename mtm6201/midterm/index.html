About Two Scoops of Happiness

Two Scoops of Happiness Ice Cream was founded by Caroline Wesbox Channing in Ottawa ten years ago. At the beginning, Caroline was a single mom who decided to focus on the ice cream shop full time. Five years later, she managed to move her business into a small ice cream store. Two Scoops of Happiness Ice Cream is now located on 1866 Hinton Ave, Ottawa.
